Rico 1: The Quest Begins

A thrilling adventure from Phantom Games

Join Rico, a brave red slime cube, on a thrilling platforming adventure inspired by classic Mario-style games! Jump, bounce, and defeat enemies across colorful levels filled with mystery boxes, coins, and surprises. Unlock power-ups like fireballs, dodge tricky traps, and conquer each stage in your quest to reach the goal. With retro pixel art and smooth controls, this action-packed journey is perfect for fans of old-school platformers!

Key Features:

  • Engaging storyline with multiple endings
  • Dynamic combat system
  • Stunning handcrafted environments
  • Challenging puzzles and riddles
  • Rich lore and character development

Get ready to define your legend in Rico 1!
